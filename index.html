Biography

I am a Post-Doctoral researcher at the Image and Visual Representation Lab (IVRL) in EPFL, Lausanne. I am also going to be local arrangement chair for the next edition of ICCP in Lausanne, for which you can find information here. My main research interests are computer vision, image processing, and the statistical modelling of natural images. A list of my publications with filters is here which you can also find on my Google Scholar. Feel free to visit my Github page, where I release the code associated to my projects.
